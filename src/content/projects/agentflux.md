# Agentflux

AgentFlux is a next-generation real estate platform designed to replace outdated, fragmented legacy tools.
It unifies lead management, property data, client interactions, and AI-driven automation into a single streamlined system.

Instead of juggling CRMs, spreadsheets, and third-party services, AgentFlux centralizes the entire real estate workflow—capturing leads, qualifying them with AI,
generating property descriptions automatically, and connecting agents to clients in real time.
It represents the shift from slow, manual, disconnected systems to a fluid, intelligent, and adaptive platform built for modern real estate.
